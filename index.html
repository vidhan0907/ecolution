Introduction

Welcome to Ecolution 2.0! Ecolution 2.0 is a flagship event organized by the IAYP Club of Sri Sri Academy, Kolkata. Our mission is to promote peace, justice, and strong institutions through various engaging activities. This event aims to inspire and empower students to express their creativity and passion for social change. Join us as we embark on this journey of learning, collaboration, and innovation.
